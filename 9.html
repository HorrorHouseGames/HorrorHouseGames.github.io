      How to Play

      Navigate your spaceship through the cosmos to explore and gather resources.

      • Move: Move your mouse or drag your finger to guide the ship.
      • Shoot: Right-click or double-tap to fire lasers (red, 1 damage). Lasers auto-fire every 0.5s when not docked or menu open.
      • Destroy Asteroids: Hit asteroids to spawn 3–5 reward squares (yellow: item, cyan: tokens, purple: materials, green: XP).
      • Collect Rewards: Move over reward squares to collect them.
      • Dock: Approach special particles (planets, space station) within 50px to dock, earning an item and 5 Loyalty Tokens. Move 100px away to undock.
      • View Menu: Click/tap your ship when not docked to open the ship menu. Close by clicking the "Close" button or outside the menu.
      • Explore Content: Click/tap special particles to view HorrorHouseGames content.

      Possible Rewards

      Collect rewards from asteroids and docking.

      • Common: Fuel Cell, Repair Kit (50% chance)
      • Uncommon: Shield Booster, Speed Upgrade (30% chance)
      • Rare: Laser Enhancer, Stealth Coating (15% chance)
      • Epic: Quantum Drive, Red Glow Skin, Nebula Trail (5% chance)
      • Resources: Space Tokens, Raw Materials, Loyalty Tokens, XP

      Leveling System

      Gain XP to level up your profile.

      • XP Requirement: 100 XP per level (e.g., Level 1: 0–99 XP, Level 2: 100–199 XP).
      • Earning XP: Collect green XP reward squares (10 XP each) from asteroids.
      • Level Display: Check your current level in the Profile tab.
